# Olist E-commerce Analysis

Analysis of 100,000+ orders from a Brazilian marketplace (2016-2018).

## What I Did
- Cleaned and validated data from 9 interconnected tables
- Answered 8 business questions using SQL
- Validated all findings against Excel
- Delivered actionable recommendations

## Key Findings
- **Only 5.6% of customers made a second purchase** — major retention opportunity
- **Health/beauty** accounts for 9.2% of total profit
- **Monday 10am-4pm** are peak hours for purchases
- **November (Black Friday)** shows significant sales spike

## Recommendations
- Increase marketing on health/beauty and watches/gifts
- Discontinue or discount CDs/dvds, security/services, children's clothes
- Schedule campaigns for Monday mornings and November
- Implement a retention strategy (loyalty program, post-purchase emails)
