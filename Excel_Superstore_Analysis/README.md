# Superstore Sales Analysis with Excel

This dataset analyses the Superstore sales dataset to identify revenue trends and top selling products and categories using Microsoft Excel

# Key Questions

Which product categories produce the most revenue?

Technology produces the most revenue, marginally ahead of office supplies. Both supply most of the revenue whilst furniture only produces a small fraction of
total revenue

Which sub-categories perform best?

Chairs and phones generate the highest amount of sales by a considerable margin compared to other sub-categories. Copiers has the highest ratio of profit to sales
distinguishing itself from other sub categories. Tables, bookcases and supplies have cost the business in profit, with tables generating high sales but loss of profit.

# Insights
Furniture generates less than 10% of total profit for the business, with Technology generating the highest profit.

Furniture has the highest amount of sales by a considerable margin compared to the other categories, which suggests a lower value to the business as a whole.

Copiers have the highest value to the business, as seen as having a very high profit to sales ratio.
