## Featured Project: E-Commerce Retention Audit
**The Problem:** An e-commerce marketplace with 100,000+ orders was struggling with customer loyalty.  
**The Insight:** My analysis revealed that **94% of the customer base** never returned for a second purchase.  
**The Solution:** I identified specific friction points in the customer journey and product categories with the highest churn, providing a roadmap to reclaim lost revenue.
